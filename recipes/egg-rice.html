Egg Rice

Description

To make egg rice, start by cooking 2 cups of rice and letting it cool so the grains remain separate; heat oil or butter in a pan, add finely chopped onions and sauté until translucent, then add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies and cook until fragrant. Push the onions to the side, crack in 3-4 eggs, season with salt and pepper, scramble them lightly, then mix with the onions. Add vegetables like carrots, beans, or capsicum if desired and stir-fry briefly, then add the cooked rice along with soy sauce (or salt to taste) and a pinch of black pepper, mixing gently on high heat so the rice doesn't break. Finish with spring onions or coriander leaves, adjust seasoning, and serve hot on its own or with a side of chili sauce or raita.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cooled)
  • 3-4 eggs
  • 2 tbsp oil or butter
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped (optional)
  • ½ cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, capsicum - optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1-2 tsp soy sauce (optional)
  • Spring onions or coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the rice until done, spread it on a plate, and allow it to cool so the grains remain separate.
  2. Heat oil or butter in a pan or wok over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until soft and translucent.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies; sauté until fragrant.
  5. Push the onion mixture to one side, crack in the eggs, add a pinch of salt and pepper, and scramble them lightly.
  6. Mix the scrambled eggs with the onion mixture.
  7. Add vegetables (if using) and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes.
  8. Add the cooked rice and toss gently on high heat.
  9. Add soy sauce (if using), salt, and black pepper; mix well.
  10. Cook for another 1-2 minutes, garnish with spring onions or coriander, and serve hot.
